Demand a Debate

Stand with us and demand that our political candidates address issues affecting women and girls during their debates by voting on and/or submitting a question below.


Economic Opportunities: What can the federal government do to financially ease the burden on working parents trying to balance career and child care?

Health Care: How might we mobilize religious and non-religious resources to collaborate and address the tragedy of abortion by reducing unplanned (and unwanted) pregnancies?

Personal Safety: How might we better safeguard young women (e.g., through stronger laws, working with law enforcement agencies) to reduce the incidence of sexual assault on our university campuses?
