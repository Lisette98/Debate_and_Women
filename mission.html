Mission

Who We Are

The Women’s Debate serves as a platform to convene a collective voice and facilitate questions for our political candidates. This platform will generate solutions proposed by these candidates to advance women in three areas: safe and just recourse for women when it comes to violence in the home, in the military, in prisons and on campus (Personal Safety); removal of barriers to full participation in the workplace (Economic Opportunities), and access to affordable and quality health services to ensure marginalized female populations are taken care of (Healthcare).

Together, we are raising awareness about women’s rights in the lead up to the 2016 federal election. The Women’s Debate calls on all political candidates to commit to a presidential debate on issues impacting women, namely:

  • Safe and just recourse for women when it comes to violence;
  • Reduction of barriers that inhibit equitable participation in the economy; and
  • Access to quality affordable health care opportunities for all.

See About Section for more information, and join us! For questions, please contact info@womensdabate.com.

It is our vision that gender safety and equality becomes a reality in all spheres of public and private life.
