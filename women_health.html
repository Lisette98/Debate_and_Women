Women's Health

New attention to women's health issues in recent decades has produced heartening progress in fighting breast cancer, osteoporosis and other diseases. Other issues that impact women include family planning and maternal care. The Affordable Healthcare Act (ACA) reforms remain unavailable to women for whom health insurance itself is out of reach.
